The Time I Accidentally Joined a Goat Parade

It started like any other Sunday. I went out for a walk with headphones in, lost in my own world — until I noticed a group of goats trotting down the street. Naturally, I followed them. Who wouldn’t?

Five minutes later, I found myself in the middle of a local farmer’s **annual goat parade**, surrounded by kids (both goats and humans), a guy in a chicken suit, and someone playing the accordion. A tourist took a photo of me and asked how long I’d been a “goat guide.”

I didn’t have the heart to tell him I was just trying to get to the grocery store.
